"What's Hot Tonight" is designed to direct users to the hottest music shows of the night in their local city. Volunteer Track
GitHub
 
Live
"Volunteer Track" is designed to simplify tracking volunteer hours for institutions. Many schools mandate that parents volunteer for a required number of hours per year. Parents and staff are still doing this tracking on paper or within the Office suite. VolunteerTrack moves the management of these vital hours to a web-based service and provides an administrator with the ability to verify and confirm activity hours.
